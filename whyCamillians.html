FAQs

WHAT IS HOME HEALTH?

Home health services are offered to those who have difficulty or limitations in leaving their home to find/secure medical assistance. Skilled nursing, physical therapy, speech therapy, occupational therapy, or home health aides are all available. These services are often provided to those who request home health assistance. All our home health patients will remain under a physician’s care, supervision, and observation.

HOW MUCH DOES HOME HEALTH COST?

Medicare
This is for patients 65 years or older. Benefits are typically covered at 100%.
